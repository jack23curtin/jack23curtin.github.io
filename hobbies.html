Surfing
I was about 8 when I first started to learn how to surf. My first board was a 6’ Softtop with blue stars all over it. When I first started learning, I’ll admit it wasn’t my favorite thing to do. Now I can’t get out enough. I try to go at least once a week on the weekends. My favorite places to surf are Pleasure Point, Tourmaline, and Rincon.
