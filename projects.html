Pulse Website - (Spring 2017)
 Project Lead
Our team had the idea to put the game of Truth or Dare online with users paying others to complete the dares. What I loved about this project was that it was a culmination of all my high school computer science courses, everything from graphic design to database management. What I am most proud of is the fact that we were able to accomplish almost all our goals: Login system, ability to submit dares, and view videos. The only thing we could not accomplish was the monetary aspect of the website. The most difficult part of the project was that since I was a project lead, I had to delegate tasks to the individuals, some of which did not want to participate. However, once I explained that since it was a group project and if one person did not pull their weight would affect us all, the peer would usually participate again.
